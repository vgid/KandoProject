My Mission
To introduce children from rote learning environments to opportunities for creative learning and problem solving, through the use of low cost tools and technology.

My Vision
A natural incorporation of low cost technologies, resources and learning tools into existing rote learning environments, to help children achieve creative and entrepreneurial success throughout their educational careers.

About Me
A former social media marketing coordinator, online advertiser and consultant, now a technology and education researcher working with various Non-Governmental Organizations and MIT's Media Lab.

Sunday, December 13, 2009
Sanjli Gidwaney

I'm sitting on the couch, happy to complete another workshop at the MIT Museum. Today was a test of Scratchlets, a tangible version of Scratch software; a learning tool designed by the Lifelong Kindergarten group at MIT's Media Lab. I originally created Scratchlets as a precursor to Scratch, to teach children with limited access to computers the principles and function of Scratch software.
