My Mission
To introduce children from rote learning and developing communities, to opportunities for creative learning and problem solving using low cost tools and technology which foster such life skills.

My Vision
My vision is that low cost technologies, resources and tools are incorporated into rote learning systems of education so as to help children achieve the pinnacle of creative and entrepreneurial skills.

About Me
A former social media marketing coordinator, online advertiser and consultant, now a technology and education researcher working with various Non-Governmental Organizations and MIT's Media Lab.
